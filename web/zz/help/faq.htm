Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions. Use them as guidelines while operating the Online Moving Software.

Q. Which system tables should I setup first?
A. We recommend you to setup the following tables as part of the basic setup operations: Setup company info, Setup prices, Users Table, Remarks text, Update email center, and Price per hour by states.
Q. Where/How do I enter new users to the system?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Table Update--->Users Table.
Q. How do I edit the "Printed Remarks on the Quote"?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Table Update--->Remarks text. (More help available there)
Q. Where do I create emails & subjects?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Table Update--->Update Email Center. (More help available there)
Q. Where do I change the Advertising Table?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Table Update--->Advertising Table.
Q. Where do I setup local moves rate?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Table Update--->Price Per Hour By States/cities.
Q. Where do I change the estimate calculation from CF to LBS?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Setup Prices--->Basic Setup (1).
Q. Where/How do I replace follow-up estimates from user to user?
A. Go to Maintenance--->Replace Follow-up Users. (Recommended once the user is no longer active or not working in the company)
It's also possible to replace estimate between users through
Estimate in Process--->Reassign Estimates.
Q. I entered a wrong payment. How do I delete/correct it?
A. A payment can not be deleted. Instead create another payment transaction and put the payment amount in negative. This will cancel the wrong transaction. Now enter the correct payment amount.
Q. Can I/customer change the inventory once the estimate is booked?
A. No, the customer can't update his inventory once the estimate is booked. The assigned user needs to  "release book" to update it.
Q. Why can't I enter two estimates with the same email? How can I bypass it?
A. In order to prevent duplicate estimates and conflicts between users and the customer, the system will not create a second estimate with the same email. In order to bypass it, mark the first estimate "VIP" in the charges screen, located above the follow-up date.
Q. I have Estimators. Why can't I assign them to visual estimates?
A. A user, who is an estimator, has to be marked as an estimator in the users table.
Q. Why after printing the Moving Quote it comes out cut on the right side?
A. The page setup on the browser can be adjusted. On the browser
go to File--->Page Setup--->Set the Margins.
Left: 0.25, Right: 0.25, top: 0.25, bottom: 0.25
Q. How do I adjust my Internet Explorer browser's settings?
A. Open Internet Explorer. Go to--->Tools--->Internet options
--->Settings--->Mark the option "Every visit to the page".
This action is recommended when working over the Internet.
