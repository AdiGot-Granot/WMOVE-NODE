Agents

Agents or Carriers is a term known in the moving industry for a service provided by a third party company (Moving Company, Trailer Driver or Owner/Operator). Moving Companies use Agents/Carriers to deliver customers household goods for them based on financial agreement. Moving Brokers sell or broker out jobs (household goods pick-ups or car shipping) to Agents/Carriers.

Create a you own list of Agents/Carries. Click on Add Agent from the left menu and fill out the information of the Moving Company you're adding.

Assigning an Agent to a job is done through the job's Charges or Operations screen (depend on Moving Software package compatibility).

Click submit at the end to save any changes made.
