Phone Dialer
by
Gako Communication

Gako Communication has developed an auto dialer to improve your outbound calls productivity.

Sales Representatives using the dialer can triple the amount of outbound calls typically made daily and will focus an operator’s time with calls answered by a live person, maximizing performance and efficiency. Dialed calls that reach a busy signal will be automatically passed over and logged for redial, while calls that reach voicemail will be left a customized pre-recorded message, with a marked log for follow up as well.

The Dialer drives telephone agents to commit to the campaign of loaded leads by calling the customers phone numbers one by one. As soon as an agent wraps-up a call, the dialer will retrieve and automatically dial the next number on the loaded list.

The advantage Gako Dialer has is its’ integration with your Online Moving Software platform. Sales Representatives can access and review actual customer information concurrently as calls are being initiated or occurring.

The Gako Dialer can be used from any POTS (Plain old telephone service), ATA (analog telephone)/ VoIP service, or cell connection.
