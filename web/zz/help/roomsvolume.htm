Rooms Volume:

Setup the Volume/Weight of each bedroom or office move description. It appears under Select Number of Rooms when entering New Estimates by data entry.

The result is a quick, ballpark estimate based on the size of move.

As customary in any type of move, ask the customer for a detailed inventory list or send the customer an email to fill it out on his own.

Selecting a move size description is located on the Entry Form when creating a New Estimate.
