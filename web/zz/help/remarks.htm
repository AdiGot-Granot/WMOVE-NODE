Text Remarks on Estimate

Write your company Terms & Conditions, which are presented to the Customer/Shipper on the Printed Estimate form in Understanding Your Estimate part. It is important to add/update your company Terms & Conditions as part of your initial Moving Software setup. Any text update will be effective from the next new estimate.

You can create different Terms & Conditions for different:

Type of Moves: Local/Long Distance/International/Auto Transport
Status of Job:   Estimate/Booked
Binding Type:   Binding/Non-Binding
Departments:    Applies with multi companies/departments feature
Type of Service: Residential/Commercial/Military

Terms & Conditions will apply on every new estimate based on the definition you have created.
Click Preview to see the related text definition. Click Update to update the text for the related definition.

What do I write? Tips and pointers.

Owning or operating a Moving Company or Moving Broker, you should have an outline of your company policy for your customers.

1. Company services.
2. Services/materials included and not included in the estimate.
3. Write if the estimate is binding or non-binding.
4. Payment and cancellation policy.
5. Delivery policy.
6. Write if you're a Broker or a Carrier.
7. Write about insurance and claim for damages process.
8. Add links to "Your Rights & Responsibilities When You Move" & "Ready to Move” Brochure. Links can be found through Google search or ProtectYourMove.gov website.
