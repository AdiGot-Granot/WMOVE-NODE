What is Equote?

Equote is an option to send the Moving Estimate/Order for Service as a link using a short introductory email in plain text.

The reason the email is in plain text (no logo, coloring, and no special fonts) is to minimize the probability of the email going into spam or junk. Email companies (Aol, Gmail, Hotmail, Yahoo) keep increasing their email security and protocols for their customers. Emails carrying an image, colored text, or even links are susceptible to being labeled as SPAM.

The short introductory email provides the customer with a link to the Moving Estimate. That enables the system to record it in Recurring Customers, notifying you which customer actually viewed the Moving Estimate.

To enable the option of sending an Equote, simply click on the check box. This will present the Equote check box in the email center.
