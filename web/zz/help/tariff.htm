Custom Tariff Solution

 As of January1, 2008 all Moving Companies are required to publish their own TARIFF.

Granot Inc, the creators of the original Moving Management Software, developed for you a Custom Tariff Solution.

You can create and post your private moving tariff, (price list of charging your costumers), and intergrades it with your Moving Management Software. The moving tariff can be posted in volume or in weight.

Please note:
You are not obligated to enter your tariff. The current tariff is still operational and is FREE to use. It's your responsibility to check with the authorities in your state if you need to submit it for approval.
**In case you own a special made tariff, we would need to review it before adding it to your system. Please call our office for information and pricing.

The Moving Management Software offers a Custom Tariff Solution package:

1. Build your company tariff in easy three steps:
    a. Specify your yearly season period. (no limit)
    b. Specify your volume scale. (i.e., 0-200, 201-450, etc')
    c. Categorize your states line of operation. All states are required. In addition, you
        can pinpoint a zip and radius as a specify line of operation zone.

2. The rate form provides fast pricing tools such as:
    a. Copy of prices from zone to zone.
    b. Setup volume rates based on add/reduce percent scale.

3. Upon price changes, you can create a new tariff. The previous tariff will be saved as a
    record for review.

4. Create/define a list of additional services, given by the company, which add an
    additional charge to the estimate. (i.e, long carry, pickup fee, delivery fee, extra stop,
    piano, stairs/elevator, shuttle service)

5. Create/define a list of discounts, given by the company. (i.e., senior citizen, coupon,
    recurring customer)
