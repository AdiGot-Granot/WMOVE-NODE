Price per Hour by States: Local & Intrastate Move

Setup rates per hour for Local and Intrastate Moves. Indicate price per hour for 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 men and your minimum price. You can still make adjustments per estimate, when applicable.

1. Select state from a dropdown list.
2. Write your rates per hour for sending 2-6 Men.
3. Write your minimum price.
4. Intrastate Move: Indicate number of miles from Origin ZIP code, from which a charge per mile will apply (Per Mile cell).
5. Write how much you charge Per Mile* on intrastate moves.

* Fuel & Mileage (on Charges screen) will be updated accordingly.

The price per hour will be taken from this setup table when a Local Move estimate is created. It is your responsibility to update this table before creating local move estimates.
