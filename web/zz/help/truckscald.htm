Trucks Calendar

Trucks Calendar is built to assist the Dispatch knowing which of the company's Trucks is occupied picking up or delivering jobs and which one is available.
The Dispatch can also indicate how many days a Truck is traveling on the road making it unavailable.
To best utilize this calendar, you assign a Truck to a pick-up or delivery job and write the travel days (optional).

The Trucks Calendar will not produce a result without first setting up Truck(s) in the Trucks Table.

In the subject of using a third party Agent/Carrier to deliver jobs, you can open  a Trip (different function).
