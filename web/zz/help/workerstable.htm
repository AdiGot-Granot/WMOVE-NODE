Workers Table

List your company Workers or field employees in order to enable the option of assigning a crew to a job. Assigning a crew to a job is done by accessing the Operations tab. You can assign a crew to every operational phase such as Box or Packing Delivery, Pick-Up and Delivery.
Assigning workers to a job also enables the option of wages or paying a salary based on the commission criteria provided.

Click on "Add Worker" from the Workers Table and fill out the Worker information.

Click submit to save any changes that you made.
