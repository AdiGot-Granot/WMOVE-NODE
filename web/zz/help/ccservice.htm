Credit Card Processing Integration

Credit card processing is an easy and secured way to charge your customers credit card. Payments go directly to the bank account you setup with your Merchant provider.

How does it work?

1. Check with your Merchant provider if you can process credit cards through the following integrated credit card processing Gateways: Authorize.net and/or Fidelity Payment Systems.
2. Setup the Merchant Services page with the required credentials: API Log and Transaction Key.

How to start?

First, you need to setup a Merchant account. Contact the credit card processing Gateways below. These are the ones integrated with the Moving Software.
