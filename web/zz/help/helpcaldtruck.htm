About Trucks Calendar


Trucks calendar gives the dispatcher the option to see which truck is occupied picking up or delivering a job/s and how many days the truck will be occupied.
In order to work properly with this calendar you need to assign a truck to a job and write how many days it will be occupied. This action can be performed from the job itself.

This screen will not produce a result unless prior setup in the trucks table was made. In case you are not getting the desired result ask your self the following questions:

1. Did you setup truck records in the trucks table?
2. Was the truck assigned to the job/s?

Click submit at the end to get the desired results.
