Setup Company Info

Setup your company's information and profile. This information appears on the Moving Estimate (Printed Estimate) page. It's important to review this page after your account is setup in order to correct or add any missing information.

If you have the Multi-Department Add-On feature, the company information there has priority over this one. Make sure you use and update your company info under the Department Table.

Estimate Subject: email subject of your Moving Estimate email.
Book Subject: email subject of your Booked Job email.
Email Subject: email subject of your automatic first email letter (if apply).
Web Redirect: relevant when you connect your website with a Free Quote form posted to the Moving Software. The link redirects your customers to your own "thank you" web page which can contain any type of tracking scripts your webmaster have prepared.

Electronic Signature Message
Your customers can sign and confirm their move online via a link on the Moving Estimate. Utilize the Electronic Signature message to present your customers with your Terms and Conditions. The following can/should include your cancellation policy, payment and refund, delivery policy etc'.

Thank You Message to Customer After Inventory
A message that your customers will get after accessing their estimate and updating their inventory.
