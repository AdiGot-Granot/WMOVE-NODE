Data Cleanup

Clean your data from non-potential leads and keep your Moving Software working faster and more efficient. Non-potential lead can be any follow up or cancelled lead with past move date.

Follow the available Data Cleanup options:

 1. Canceled estimates
 2. Follow-up estimates with past move date.
 3. Closed jobs.
 4. Booked jobs.
 5. Book jobs with Agent/Carrier

You can retrieve estimates that been cleaned up through Search by indicating the Search in History checkbox with your search parameter (job number, customer name and email).

Please be responsible with your selection.
