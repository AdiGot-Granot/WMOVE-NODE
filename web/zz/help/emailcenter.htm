Email Center

You can create a variety of Email Subjects and Letters that every User, who takes part in the sales process and/or customer service can send to customers.
It is recommended to create Email Subjects and Letters as part of your initial Moving Software setup.

Popular Email Subjects:
1. Update you inventory.
2. Follow up on your move.
3. Company Information.
4. Unable to reach you.
5. Credit card authorization form.

Every email you create has the "Update Inventory" link checked on and attached to it. It's also compatible with HTML. You can use the Html Editor link to edit your emails. Click Preview to view your email.

You can add Keyword Tags of information from the estimate to the email, assign specific emails to departments or type of moves and setup an Email Center for the subject of Job Claim. These options are subject to Moving Software package compatibility.

Email Center is located on the Charges screen by clicking on the customer's email.

Click submit to save any changes made.
