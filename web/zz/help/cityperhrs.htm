Price per Hour by Cities: Local & Intrastate Move

Setup your Local and Intrastate Move pricing by indicating how much you charge per hour in selected cities within the states you are working locally. This will allow you to setup different hourly rates per city. (You can still adjust the rate per estimate).

1. Select state and city.
2. indicate the zip code and radius surrounding the city.
3. Write your pricing for sending 2-6 Men.
4. Write your minimum price (Local min).

Repeat these steps as necessary.

The rates per hour indicated on this setup screen will have priority over the Price per Hour by States when the information on the Local Move estimate matches with your setup.

It is your responsibility to setup this table before creating/purchasing local move leads.
