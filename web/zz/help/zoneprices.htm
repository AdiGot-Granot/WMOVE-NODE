Zone Prices - Setup Your Tariff Discounts

Setting up your tariff discounts is the second step in the process that deals with the long distance Tariff. All figures on this section are in percent values.

You can setup different periods to the yearly tariff. Each period with its own discount percent rate. The zones are divided into three sections:

1. Move between undefined regions: Moves from areas you didn't pre-set on the Zone Table. Meaning, you're not operating there. Usually the discount percent will be low ergo, the rate and price for the move will be expensive.

2. Move between defined regions to undefined regions: Moves from one defined area (zone) to anywhere. Meaning, you're operating either in the pick up or the delivery area. Usually the discount percent will moderate, the rate and price will be economic enough to perform it.

3. Move between defined regions: Moves where both areas of pick ups and deliveries are within your line of operation. Usually the discount percent will moderate to high, the rate and price will be attractive enough to perform it.
