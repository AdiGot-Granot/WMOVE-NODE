Storage Table:

Setup Storage facilities, warehouse or units that you use to store shippers' household items until delivery. Storage name and info can be the location of the storage. The company mailing address is presented on the Storage Invoice for customers to mail in their monthly payments.

Each Storage facility can have its own Storage Invoice Terms and Conditions. Utilize the link provided to update it.

Click on "Add storage" to create storage. This will enable you to insert customers into storage as part of the operation and job completion.

Click submit to save any changes made.
