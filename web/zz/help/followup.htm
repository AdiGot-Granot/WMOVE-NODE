Follow-Up Menu

This screen lists all your follow-up estimates that were assigned to you to handle, contact customers and eventually book as part of the sales process. It's your responsibility is to organize your follow up estimates by the follow up date. It's important to be one step ahead while working on your follow up list. Always set a future follow up date when you need to contact a customer next. Having follow up estimates with past follow up date means that you're not changing the date after handling a customer's estimate.

Priorities
Another tool to arrange your follow up estimates is by using the priority option. You can do it by clicking on the word Priority on the Charges screen and select the relevant priority. You have 8 priorities to choose from. Feel free to define for yourself what each priority mean to you.

Change Priority Numbers:
Easy access tool to change a bulk of follow-up estimates from one priority number to another.
