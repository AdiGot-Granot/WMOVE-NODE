Outgoing Mail Server

Outgoing mail server (SMTP) is an email protocol that sends emails. Your email hosting provider provides you with that once you purchase email accounts from it.

The Moving Software send emails from a built-in SMTP Mail Server or by connecting to your business emails when the Outgoing Mail Server option is activated. This option can help with reducing emails going to spam.

You can setup 5 different outgoing emails accounts. Option is offered for free Or go with the Premium Outgoing Email Account with and option to add up to 25 business email accounts.

How do I set it up?
1. Make sure you have your SMTP outgoing mail server, email accounts and passwords.
2. Enter the information and click SAVE.
3. Enter any email address in the EMAIL TO to test it.
4. Click on the TEST EMAIL link to test each account.
5. Check if you received the test email.
6. Activate the email account who sent the test email.

Click save to save any changes made.
