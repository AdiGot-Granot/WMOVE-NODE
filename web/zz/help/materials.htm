Materials Table

Setup your list of Packing Materials. You can price each material separately for Local and Long Distance Move in addition to adding, updating or deleting any material as necessary. The Cost column is to indicate the cost of buying the material from a Packing Materials Supplier/Wholesaler

Pricing Packing Materials for Local Move generally includes just the cost of the material to the customer. It does not including the time it takes the Mover to use it in the process of packing.

Pricing Packing Materials for Long Distance Move generally includes the cost of the material to the customer and the time it takes the Mover to use it in the process of packing.

Use the Packing Material link on the Charges screen to input the quantity of each material the customer needs. You can do the same on the Job Completion screen after the job was performed. (subject to Moving Software package).

Important: How does the Customer see the list?

You can indicate in Setup Prices whether to show the customer your entire Packing Materials list and pricing or Just show the customer the Packing Materials he choose.

Working and adding packing materials on estimates will produce a sold & profit reports.
