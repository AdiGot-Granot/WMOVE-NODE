Trucks Table

List your company trucks in order to enable the option to assign a Truck to a job. Assigning a Truck(s) to a job is done by accessing the Operations tab. You can assign a Truck to every operational phase such as Box or Packing Delivery, Pick-Up and Delivery. If a job is scheduled for more then one day, you can indicate the duration (in days) the truck will be occupied. This will be reflected in the Truck Calendar as you can see there which Truck is occupied and on which job.

Click on "Add truck" from the Trucks Table and fill out the Truck information.

Click submit to save any changes that you made.
