Recurring Customers

Recurring Customers records which of your customers received an email containing a link to their estimate file and clicked on it.

You can include a link on your email templates (default setup) that will direct a customer to access his estimate and update his inventory list.

This recording action will alert you in order to attend to these customers first. Recurring Customers also records customers accessing their estimate to electronically sign it and also a link to a friendly way to view their estimate in a new page (Equote).

Please note: customers who opened their email but didn't access their file will not be recorded. Customers must click on their estimate number. Also, those who click it may not necessarily mean they perform the requested update.
