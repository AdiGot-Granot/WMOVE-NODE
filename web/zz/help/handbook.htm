Handbook & Workflow

Create call scripts and workflow procedures to help your sales and customers service team engage better with customers.

Teach them how to answer an inbound call and how to make an outbound one. Show them how to take inventory and the workflow of booking a job. Provide them with a price list for accessorial services (stairs, elevator, long carry etc.). Payment and cancellation policies.

By having a knowledgeable sales team you can reach the target of booking jobs. By having a professional customer service team you build trust with your customers to get positive feedback and reviews.
