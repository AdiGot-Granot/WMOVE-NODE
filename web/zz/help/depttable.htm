Department Table

This feature enables you manage several departments of the company including a DBA (Doing Business As) of the company. You can separate the departments in a way that each location or company gets access to work and see its own estimates. Each department will have its own letter(s) code. Additional separation is done by changing the group number.

Utilizing the Department Table allows you to assign each User to a department, have a separate Company Policy, Email Center and Leads Grabbing.

Utilizing the group number separation will ensure that Users can only work on their assigned department(s), unless you give the View All Estimate privilege (Users Table).

Create a new department by click on Add Dept from the left menu and fill out the information of the company.

* Important: You must fill out and submit to Granot Inc a Confirmation of Ownership form in case you act as a Booking Agent or operate under different company names.

Click submit to save any changes that you made.
