Zone Table - Areas of Operation

Setting up your areas of operation is the first step in the process that deals with the long distance Tariff.

Here you can define the areas you perform pick ups and deliveries. You have a get a default setup covering major cities and states with a zip code and radius.

You can make changes to match with your areas of operation. Utilize the map image and click on a state to view the state's outline, main cities and zip codes.

Click submit to save any changes that you made.
