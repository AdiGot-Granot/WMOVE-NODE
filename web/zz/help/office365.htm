How to enable SMTP Client Authentication for the Tenant

Office 365 domain email customers need to enable SMTP Client Authentication in order to integrate and send emails.

If you received the email message "535 EAUTH 535 5.7.139 Authentication unsuccessful, SmtpClientAuthentication is disabled for the Tenant.", follow these steps to enable it:
  1. Login to Your GoDaddy account: https://productivity.godaddy.com
  2. Select Manage next to the User.
  3. Scroll down to Account information and select Advanced Settings.
  4. Turn on the SMTP Authentication toggle.
  5. Select Continue to confirm you want to enable SMTP Authentication for this user.
  6. Repeat these steps for every email that you want to integrate on your Moving Software.
