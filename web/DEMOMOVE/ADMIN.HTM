A moving company is a company that assists people and businesses relocate their personal belongings from one location to another.

For long distance moves, the cost is generally determined by the weight or the size of the items needing to be moved and the mileage. For local moves, the cost usually depends on the number of hours it takes to move the goods.

Moving companies usually receive moving leads in this manner business, and the consumer may get a moving quote for a lower price because the companies compete for business; however, leads provides charge the moving companies for the leads.
